About Us

Who is Alpha Movers?

We started a while ago with a team of only two members and 1 truck. In a short time of 4 years we have grown from just 2 members to a family of 40+ members and 15 trucks providing services in Victoria, SA, NSW and QLD. All of this became possible because of the trust that the customers have put into us. And we try our best not to break that trust. All our team members keep the mindset of keeping our customers happy, and that is the reason that we always go that extra mile for you if required.

We will never forget that we started from a humble beginning and that's why we still train our team to provide the same service, full of compassion and care that enabled us to grow this fast in the first place.

This is one of the reason that our customers are always happy to endorse us.
